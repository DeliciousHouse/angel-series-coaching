Rituals create rhythm. They help you return to yourself, especially when life is busy.

## A 20-minute weekly reset

1. Review wins and lessons.
2. Choose three priorities.
3. Schedule deep work.
4. Set one self-care commitment.

### Keep it light

The best rituals are easy to maintain. Aim for consistency over perfection.
